Lesson: recharts `ticks` on a category axis silently NaN-drops values that aren't exact domain members, and a numeric `interval` prop decimates explicit ticks too — verified in node_modules before writing the component (plan-stage recharts source-check killed the numeric-axis alternative for Bar charts: bars center on `scale(t)` and bandSize infers from the min tick gap).
